About

Technology executive and IoT innovator

Building Connected Systems

Don Barnetson is a product and technology executive with deep expertise in IoT platforms, artificial intelligence, and connected systems. He currently serves as SVP of Product at Credo, where he leads product strategy for high-speed connectivity solutions powering modern data centers.

With 30 patents to his name, Don has a track record of turning complex technical challenges into market-ready products. As former CTO of Lunera Lighting, he led the development of an IoT-enabled smart lighting platform and drove innovations in LED technology that earned multiple patents.

Don has also served as a venture partner at Zorlu Ventures, evaluating investments in energy technology, smart systems, and sustainable infrastructure. His career spans hardware, software, and the systems that connect them -- always with an eye toward the transformative potential of IoT and AI.
